# DupZero Privacy Policy
## Developed by Tavoo
## Last Updated: 2024

---

DupZero ("the App") is developed by Tavoo ("Developer").
This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

---

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored on Your Device Only
- File paths and hashes (for duplicate detection)
- Scan history
- App settings and preferences
- Deleted files (Recycle Bin — 30 days)

### Data Sent to Our Servers
- Email address (if you create an account)
- Subscription status (for Pro features)

### Data We NEVER Collect
- Your actual file contents
- Photos or videos
- Personal documents
- Location data
- Contacts
- Browsing history

---

## How We Use Your Data

- File hashes: To detect duplicate files on your device
- Email: To manage your account and send important updates
- Subscription status: To unlock Pro features

---

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your data with:
- Advertisers
- Data brokers
- Third-party companies

The only third-party service we use is:
- **Firebase (Google)**: For authentication and cloud sync
  - Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

---

## Advertising

**DupZero contains NO advertisements.**
We are funded entirely by Pro subscriptions.

---

## Data Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- Firebase Security Rules prevent unauthorized access
- You can delete your account and all data at any time

---

## Your Rights

You can:
- Access your data: Settings → Account → Export Data
- Delete your data: Settings → Account → Delete Account
- Opt out of sync: Settings → Disable Cloud Sync

---

## Children's Privacy

DupZero is not directed to children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect data from children.
