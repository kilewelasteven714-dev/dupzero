## Developed by Tavoo

---

## Kwa Nini Payoneer?

Google Play inatuma pesa kwa benki tu — sio M-Pesa moja kwa moja.
Payoneer ni benki ya kimataifa inayokubali Tanzania.
Inatoa kadi ya Mastercard unayoweza kutumia ATM yoyote.

---

## HATUA 1 — Fungua Payoneer

1. Nenda: payoneer.com
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Jaza:
   - Jina kamili (kama ID yako)
   - Tarehe ya kuzaliwa
   - Nchi: Tanzania
   - Email: Gmail yako
   - Namba ya simu
4. Verify email

---

## HATUA 2 — Thibitisha Utambulisho

Payoneer inahitaji moja ya hivi:
- National ID (Kitambulisho cha Taifa)
- Passport
- Driving License
- Student ID (Mzumbe University)

Piga picha wazi — mbele na nyuma.

---

## HATUA 3 — Subiri Approval

Siku 1-3. Email itakuja: "Your account is approved!"

---

## HATUA 4 — Omba Kadi ya Mastercard

1. Payoneer dashboard → Cards → Order Card
2. Weka anwani yako Tanzania
3. Kadi inafika wiki 2-4

---

## HATUA 5 — Unganisha na Google Play

1. Google Play Console → Setup → Payments profile
2. Add payment method → Bank account
3. Payoneer → Global Payment Service → USD account
4. Payoneer itakupa:
   - Bank name: Community Federal Savings Bank
   - Account number: (yako)
   - Routing number: (ya Payoneer)
5. Weka details hizo kwenye Google Play
6. Google itathibitisha kwa kutuma $0.01

---

## Mzunguko wa Pesa

```
Mtumiaji → Google Play ($2.99)
         → Google inachukua 15% ($0.45)
         → Wewe unapata $2.54
         → Mwisho wa mwezi → Payoneer
         → Chaguo lako:
            → Toa ATM (shilingi)
            → Tuma M-Pesa (2%)
            → Lipa online na Mastercard
```

---

## Kiwango cha Chini cha Malipo

Google inatuma pesa ukiwa na $1+ tu kwenye akaunti.
Ikiwa chini ya $1 — inasubiri mwezi ujao.

---

## Ushauri

Epuka kutoa pesa kidogo kidogo — ada ya ATM ni ~$3 kwa kila withdrawal.
Subiri uwe na $20+ kabla ya kutoa.
